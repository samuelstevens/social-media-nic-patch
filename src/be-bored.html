The Bored Board

Just Be Bored.

Look, this is the hard one. If you're only going to not have anything to do for less than a minute, then just sit there. It's not that long, and it's not that hard, but it helps your attention span to just be bored for periods of time.

Some things to do:

  • Close your eyes and think about how you physically feel.
  • Look at a wall. Imagine some fancy shapes in there.
  • Doodle on some paper.
  • Sit there and just sit.

This will get easier over time. Honestly, if you're read this far, you might be done waiting for whatever it is you're waiting. Not looking at a new Instagram photo or a Facebook post is going to make a real change in your ability to focus on one thing for a longer period of time.
