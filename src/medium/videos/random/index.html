The Good Bits of YouTube

Now that you've watched some high-quality (but admittedly dense) content, think about what you saw and write some thoughts down. This will improve your retention of the material, and will encourage a more mindful consumption of content.

Some questions to consider:

  • What did this make you think about?
  • What questions do you have?
  • What were you surprised by?
  • What do you want to know more about?
