The Good Bits of YouTube

YouTube is an amazing platform that has literally more content than you could ever watch. There is an incredible amount of quality content.

Let's Learn!

The Bad Bits

However, YouTube's revenue is ad-based, meaning that they make more money when you spend more time on their platform. This of course means that videos are produced to be as addictive as possible, and YouTube's reccommendation algorithms aim to suck you in for hours at a time.

We're going to counter this by not watching videos on YouTube to avoid the dangerous sidebar of reccommended videos (see here for how to remove the sidebar using UBlock).

In addition, we're only going to watch quality, educational content, and only for short periods of time. Then, we'll actively think about what we saw and write just a couple sentences of feedback.
