Short Stories

Short stories are some of the most approachable literature authors have ever provided. They introduce characters quickly, keep you on your toes, and typically have a powerful finish that leaves you wishing for more.

In addition, there's less commitment from you as a reader to sit down and handle a 400-page novel. You can get the entire message of a short story in one sitting, and feel accomplished without dedicating hours to a story.
