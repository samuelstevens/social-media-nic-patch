What is this?

How long will you be bored for?

It's important to define how long you will spend here. We want to avoid the "hours lost" syndrome, where you look up and 3 hours have gone by.


What is this site?

This site is my "nicotine patch" for social media. I found myself spending many, many minutes on social media sites like Facebook, Reddit, Twitter, and Instagram. These "spare minutes" ended up taking hours of my life away.

I read an amazing book called Irresistible by Adam Alter that really changed my perspective on these websites. I've written a short article on it here, but the gist of it is:

Social media forms similar pathways in your brain to incredibly addictive drugs like heroin or alcohol. When the neurons along these pathways fire, they produce dopamine, just like a drug addict getting another hit. Social media produces less dopamine than heroin, but the fact that it's so similar is alarming.

This website is to help you kick the addiction. I (and others) have formed a habit of going to these websites whenever I'm bored. I wanted a site that I could go to when I was bored, but that would encourage me to use the internet mindfully. Instead of typing facebook.com into a search bar, you can come here, and get a smaller (but still effective) dose of dopamine.

You can read more about it here, or email me to talk about it!

I'm really passionate about social media addiction, and would love to hear feedback, be it positive or negative. Please let me know if this site helps, or how it could be more helpful.

Here's to a better Internet!
