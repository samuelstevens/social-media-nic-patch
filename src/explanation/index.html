The Bored Board

What's the Point of All This?

This website is to help give people who are addicted to Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Reddit, or any other platform a fighting chance.

I'm a student who spends all of my time on Reddit. Any free second I have, I'm browsing r/all to see what's happening, desperately trying to not be bored.

In line for food? Reddit.
Professor is reviewing material I already know? Reddit.
I've got 15 minutes before I have to leave for class? Reddit.
I have a paper due in an hour, but I just can't be arsed to start it? Reddit.

It's becoming (already is) a real issue for me, and I know it affects some of the people around me. And while Reddit is my digital vice, for some people it's Facebook. For others it's Clash of Clans.

In the past, we weren't concerned about smoking. How can it harm you? It seemed foolish to believe that it was anything malicious, that tobacco companies of course had your health as one of their priorities.
